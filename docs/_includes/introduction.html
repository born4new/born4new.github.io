Introduction

This data story is a creative extension of the Standford Paper entitled "A large-scale analysis of racial disparities in police stops across the United States", where the authors analysed the racial bias that some US police officers suffer from against minorities when doing police stops.

In this extension, we would like to go a step back and study the police officers in that study. In regards to racial bias, we will try to understand what makes a good cop, and what makes a bad cop?

To that end, we have come up with a formula that we call the "Good Cop, Bad Cop Formula" (a.k.a. the GCBC formula), that we will use as the basic data for our analysis: this will be the atom in our new dataset if you will.
