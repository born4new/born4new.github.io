As for the overall experience, 44% of all officers are in their first year in the line of duty (57% in their first three years, and 64% in their five years)

Now, to the genders. Most police officers are men with an overwhelming majority at 87%.

Women are represented at 6.28%, and the remainder is unknown: data has not been provided in the initial dataset.

The races distribution look disproportionate at first sight, but is actually very close to the distribution of races in the united states.
